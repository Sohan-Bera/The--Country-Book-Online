About Us

2025 The Country Book. Official Solo Initiative Project. Created by Sohan Bera.

Creator & Inspiration

"This project began with a simple but powerful curiosity: "What makes each country unique?" I've always been fascinated by geography, culture, and the beautiful diversity of our world. This website is not backed by any organization; it was created purely out of passion to build something meaningful and educational for everyone.

Knowledge should be free, especially when it brings people closer to understanding one another. The Country Book is a clean, simple space where you can explore the world alphabetically and peacefully in one place. Thank you for visiting."
— Country Book Team

How this website helps you?

Whether you are studying, travelling, or just curious, this site saves you time by providing a clear view of our amazing world. Beyond basic country information, we include incredible facts in the GlobeX section. The website receives seamless updates—just refresh the page to see the latest content.

Tips

Use the navigation menu at the top right to discover different sections. Every button opens a window to a new part of our world.
