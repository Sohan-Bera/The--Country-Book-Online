2025 The Country Book. Official Solo Initiative Project. Created by Sohan Bera.

Creator & Inspiration


"Hi, myself Sohan Bera, the mind and heart behind The Country Book. This project began with a simple but powerful curiosity: "What makes each country unique?" I've always been fascinated by geography, culture, countries and how beautiful diverse our world is. This website is not backed by any digital organization or company. It was created purely out of passion: to build something meanningful, accessible and educational for everyone.
I believe knowledge should be free, especially when it brings people closer to understanding the world and each other.This website was born from a simple frustration. Every time I wanted to learn about country-whether its name, flags, famous foods- had to search across multiple sources, and i rarely found everything in one place. So I thought, why not bring it all together?
The Country Book is my answer to that. It's a clean, simple space where you can explore the world alphabetically, peacefully and in one place. So, that's my inspiration behind developing this website.
Thank you for visiting."
-- Sohan

How this website helps you?

This project is made purely for educational and personal use, built with honesty, effort and love for knowledge. Whether you are studing, travelling, or just curious, this site helps you to save time and gives you a clear view of our amazing world. It not only shows the countries and basic information, but also includes incredible facts in the Globex section. The website receives updates and users don't need to do anything. Just refresh the page once or twice and it'll up to date automatically.
